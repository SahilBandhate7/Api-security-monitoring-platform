Section 4.2 Critical

Data Processing Liability

"...The Provider shall be solely and fully liable for any errors, hallucinations, or data leaks originating from the AI processing engine, regardless of cause or intent, with no upper limit on damages..."
analytics AI Diagnosis

The clause "no upper limit on damages" is a severe risk. Standard industry practice caps AI-related liability at 12 months of fees. This phrasing bypasses all standard insurance protections.

Action Required: Cap Liability Insert: "Liability shall be capped at 100% of the Annual Contract Value."
Action Required: Define Hallucination Define AI output as 'experimental' to mitigate direct tort liability.
Section 8.1 Medium Risk

Intellectual Property Ownership

"...All prompts, metadata, and fine-tuning weights generated during the term of this agreement shall become the joint property of the Provider and the Client..."
analytics AI Diagnosis

Joint ownership creates legal deadlock for future IP licensing. Weights and fine-tuning data should remain Client property to ensure system portability and vendor lock-in prevention.

Revision Requested: Sole Ownership Client should retain sole ownership of all weights derived from Client Data.
