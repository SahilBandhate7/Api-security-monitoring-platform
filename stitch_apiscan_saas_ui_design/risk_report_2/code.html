Clause-by-Clause Analysis

Section 14.2
CRITICAL RISK

Unlimited Indemnification

"Contractor shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Alpha Pharma from and against any and all claims, losses, damages, and expenses without limitation..."

This clause creates an uncapped financial exposure. It fails to distinguish between direct and indirect damages, exposing ClinicalAI to consequential losses that are typically uninsurable.

check_circle

Insert a liability cap equal to 2x the annual contract value.

check_circle

Explicitly exclude consequential and punitive damages.

Section 8.5
MEDIUM RISK

Intellectual Property Assignment

"All deliverables, inventions, and work product developed under this agreement shall be the sole and exclusive property of Client..."

Broad language may inadvertently include ClinicalAI's pre-existing background IP or proprietary algorithms used to generate the deliverables.

check_circle

Add a "Background IP" carve-out to protect core technology.

check_circle

Grant the client a non-exclusive license to Background IP for use of deliverables.
